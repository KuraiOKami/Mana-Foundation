Our why

We ensure Floridians in crisis feel seen, resourced, and empowered.

Mana Foundation coordinates relief and long-term recovery with dignity—blending rapid logistics, community listening, and policy advocacy.

Mission statement

Mana Foundation mobilizes people, resources, and policy to ensure families across Florida have nourishment, safe shelter, and culturally-aware support during and after crisis. We believe neighbors deserve more than temporary aid—they deserve a pathway to thrive.

Vision

Florida communities weather storms—literal and economic—because networks of care are prepared, connected, and well-resourced.

Approach

Rapid response hubs, trusted community partners, and data-informed advocacy keep services rooted locally while scaling statewide impact.

Mission pillars

Relief with dignity

Families choose the supplies and services they need. Intake is bilingual, trauma-informed, and paper-light to reduce barriers.

Preparedness

Pre-positioned food, water, generators, and technology kits mean we can activate within two hours of an alert from local officials or partner agencies.

Community leadership

Every hub maintains a resident advisory circle that informs service design, volunteer recruitment, and advocacy priorities.

Systems change

We advocate for equitable emergency funding, resilient infrastructure, and policies that protect renters and agricultural workers.
