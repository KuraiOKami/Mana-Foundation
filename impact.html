Nutrition & stability outcomes

Pantry reach

73% of guests reported the Community Pantry Network is their primary source of fresh produce. 41% live in a census tract without a full-service grocery store.

Produce rescue partnerships prevented 520,000 pounds of food waste while increasing access to culturally significant foods like yuca, plantains, and collard greens.

Household stability

Six months after receiving Mana support, 92% of families stabilized housing and 68% reported improved employment or income.

Families are paired with case managers who coordinate counseling, workforce training, and rental support.
