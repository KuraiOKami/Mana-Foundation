Take action

Generosity fuels every delivery, hotline call, and rebuilt home.

Whether you are an individual donor, a volunteer crew, or a corporate partner, there is a role for you in the Mana movement.

Donate

Every $1 donated provides $7 of relief supplies thanks to bulk purchasing and partner farms. 95% of contributions go directly to programs.

Ways to give

  • Monthly “Sustain Florida” club
  • One-time emergency response gifts
  • Corporate and church sponsorships
  • In-kind donations (food, generators, hygiene kits)

Ready to contribute?

Email hello@manafoundation.org or call (407) 555-1234.

We will provide wiring instructions, giving receipts, and real-time updates from the field.

Prefer online giving? A Stripe-powered checkout is being configured so donors can complete secure payments without leaving the site.

Volunteer

Volunteers pack hygiene kits, drive delivery routes, serve warm meals, and deploy during disasters. All shifts include training and are family-friendly.

Local opportunities

  • Warehouse sorting at Orlando HQ (daily)
  • Mobile pantry pop-ups (weekly)
  • Senior meal deliveries (flexible)

Deployment team

Join the rapid-response corps for hurricane season. Volunteers receive FEMA IS-100 training, background checks, and stipends during multi-day deployments.

Advocacy

Join the chorus for fair housing, resilient infrastructure, and responsive emergency funding. We make advocacy easy by providing talking points, trainings, and live updates from Tallahassee.

  • Sign up for action alerts
  • Attend the Florida Resilience Table

Digital infrastructure roadmap

We are preparing a lightweight stack so donations, volunteer data, and impact stories are secure and easy to manage.

Stripe

Use Stripe Checkout and Customer Portal for one-time and recurring gifts with Apple Pay / ACH support.

  • Custom success + cancel pages on manafoundation.org
  • Webhooks to confirm gifts instantly
  • Auto-issued tax receipts

Supabase

Host static assets, store volunteer/donor records, and trigger transactional emails through Supabase Edge Functions + Email.

  • Table: support_requests for hotline follow-ups
  • Table: volunteer_signups synced from forms
  • Storage: image uploads for the gallery

Hosting

Deploy this static site to Supabase Hosting (or Netlify) with CI from GitHub. Use Supabase auth if member portals are needed later.

  • Preview branches for approvals
  • Protected environment variables for API keys
