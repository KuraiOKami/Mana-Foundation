Program detail

Community Pantry Network

37 partner sites operate mobile markets, home delivery routes, and culturally-rooted classes so every county in Florida can access fresh foods and wraparound support.

Fund a pantry item Volunteer shift guide

Program snapshot

What happens at each site

  • Choice pantry lines with culturally-relevant staples
  • Nutrition classes led by bilingual community chefs
  • Benefits navigation + health screenings
  • Doorstep deliveries for seniors & immunocompromised neighbors

Where we operate

Weekly routes cover rural counties such as Glades, Hardee, Suwannee, and Jackson, while urban hubs in Orange, Hillsborough, Miami-Dade, and Duval deliver daily. Data dashboards help us rotate sites based on zip codes facing supply gaps.
