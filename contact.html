Need assistance?

If you or someone you love needs food, shelter, or emergency supplies, please call our hotline first. If it is outside operating hours, leave a voicemail or use the form below and a case manager will respond within one business day.

Hotline checklist

  • Have your zip code ready
  • Let us know dietary or mobility needs
  • Mention if you require language interpretation

Message us

Send details to care@manafoundation.org.

Include the best way to reach you, family size, and any urgent medical supplies needed so we can prioritize support.
