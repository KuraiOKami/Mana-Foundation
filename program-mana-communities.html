Program detail

Mana Communities

Micro-neighborhoods of dignified cottages give neighbors exiting homelessness a safe home, career training, and coaching to transition into long-term housing within 12 months.

Sponsor materials Partner on land or services

Program blueprint

Built for stability

  • Clusters of 8–12 net-zero cottages with shared kitchens + laundry
  • Trauma-informed design, secure storage, pet-friendly policies
  • On-site case management, behavioral health, and legal navigation

Road to success

  • Trade apprenticeships (solar, carpentry, culinary, tech)
  • Financial coaching + savings match up to $2,000
  • Housing placement support + alumni mentorship
Phase 1 build fund 41% funded

$1.23M secured of the $3M goal for 24 cottages in Orlando + Tampa.
