Our promise

We respond quickly so Florida families never feel alone.

From the Panhandle to the Keys, Mana Foundation coordinates nourishment, emergency relief, and long-term recovery resources. Our mission is to ensure neighbors in crisis have immediate access to culturally-aware support and a pathway to stability.

Rapid response hubs

Warehouses across the state allow us to move food, water, and hygiene kits within hours of a request.

Community partnerships

We partner with local clinics, schools, and faith leaders so services are trusted and easy to access.

What we do

Programs built for Florida's realities.

Community Pantry Network

Mobile and pop-up pantries reduce food deserts in rural counties and deliver fresh produce to seniors.

  • 37 partner sites statewide
  • Weekly bilingual nutrition classes
  • Locally sourced produce first

Emergency Relief & Disaster Prep

When hurricanes strike, we mobilize volunteers, deploy supplies, and help families rebuild.

  • Pre-staged hurricane kits
  • Household micro-grants
  • Long-term case management

Education & Advocacy
